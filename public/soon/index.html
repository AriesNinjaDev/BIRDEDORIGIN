What is Birded?

To be entirely honest, this question has no great answer at this time. We are constantly making changes to Birded's concepts and what it will end up becoming. All we can tell you is that it will indeed be bird themed!

This game is being created by Ninjam Studios. Our company isn't known for producing games, but rather creating security products that can be accessed and used by major corporations in need of greater protection for their systems. This game is a side product for our company, and despite this fact, we are already pouring hundereds of hours into its development.
